About the server

The Rules

1. Be nice and respectful towards everyone.

2. Don't spam, post gory/nsfw images or content, advertise other servers, or make "jokes" about serious topics.

3. You have 3 "strikes" before the staff decides whether you stay or not. If you have a problem with anything or a question for the mods, PM - we'll get to you ASAP!

4. Follow and respect the purpose of each channel. Please read the sidebars on channels/descriptions above the channel for any specific channel rules!

5. English only in all chats except in the language lobbies. We have channels for Japanese, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Italian, and Tagalog/Filipino.

6. This is a SFW server. We try our best to abide by PG13 guidelines here. Any NSFW content should go into the adult channels. PM a mod to gain acces to those channels.

The roles

Newts: When you first join the server, you'll be a newt, a.k.a a new member. You'll have access to a newt-specific channel, to speak with other newts.

Premium members: Once you've been for a month in the server, and regularly active, you'll become a premium member!

Advanced micronations: If you are very active in the server you'll become an advanced micronation.

Miners/sinners: These are age specific roles. Being a miner means you're a minor, and being a sinner means you're an adult. Both roles have their own channels.

Regional roles: These are self-assignable roles to say what country/area you are from. For example: Japan.

Language roles: These are self-assignable roles that will give you access to the language lobbies. Only take them if you speak/are learning the language.

Game roles: These are self-assignable, pingable roles for playing various group videogames in this server.

Hetagamers: Gives you access to the Hetagames channel, where Hetalia games (for example: Hetaoni) are discussed.

Pronoun roles: Self-assignable, will let others quickly know your preferred pronouns.

The channels

Rules and announcements: Rules, announcements, FAQ, self-assignable roles, and intros.

Promos for homos: Promotion channel. Conversations here are not allowed and you may only promote the same thing once a month.

Chatz: Contains the main conversation lobbies, as well as positivity and venting channels.

Minors and adult chats: Once you've stated your age or age group in your intro you'll gain access to the chat for minors (miners-only) or the chat for adults (Old Folks Home).

APH: Channels for Hetalia-related conversations. We have aph, headcanons, au and OCs channels.

Creative corner: All things creative go here. We have channels for WIPs, finished art, cosplay and fanfics.

Language lobbies: We have 7 language channels in which you'll be able to speak other languages (Chinese, French, German, Japanese, Spanish, and Tagalog/Filipino).

Miscellaneous: Channels for other topics, such as selfies, food, memes, bot commands and spam, question of the day, song-sharing, and games discussion.

Voice chat: Feel free to play music, stream, or play videogames with other Hetalia fans in our voice channels!
